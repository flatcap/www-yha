Youth Hostels in the UK

This map shows the location of all the Youth Hostels in the UK.

The data (June 2013) came from the YHA and SYHA websites.

The raw data is publicly stored in a Google Fusion table.

The data is also available as a kml file for use in Google Earth, or on GPSs.
